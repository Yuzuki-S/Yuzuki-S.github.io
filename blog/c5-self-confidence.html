Self Confidence

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Mindfulness and self-awareness are very similar. I see mindfulness enables people to perceive their thoughts and emotions. Self-awareness is not only knowing your thoughts and emotions but also seeing the perception of mindfulness objectively.

Try at least one of the self awareness practices from the chapter (Body Scan, Journaling). What was your experience of it? How did you feel?

I tried journaling. Before I start writing my thoughts on paper, I thought nothing would go through my head and I was sure I would end up writing "nothing to write. What should I write here. Nothing comes to my mind". But when I started, fist thing that came to my mind was how hard JavaScript was! I even felt like I wouldn't make it through this sprint because of how much I struggled. I could not stop what I felt while I was working on this sprint. After the time was up, I felt really good as how I made it after all while I had these negative feelings. I should try this every time when I feel down from now on.

What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section?

I learnt from this section is that our emotions are not who we are. Acknowledging this fact enables us to control our feelings as they just come and go.
