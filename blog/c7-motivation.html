How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens?

Most people tend to try pursue their happiness by the following order, pleasure, passion and purpose. However, the effective strategy is seeking a higher purpose first, and it becomes a passion by working hard to fullfill it and finally it becomes a pleasure by achieving the purpose. This order allows us to have sustainable happiness.
