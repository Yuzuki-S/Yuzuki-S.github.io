Shereef's Fireside Chat

- My reflection after watching Shereef's Fireside Chat

What's your take on the DBC/EDA experience? It only works when we have an attitude of trying to make this a fantastic experience. We have a short time frame to learn as much as possible. EDA is all about learning as much as possible while maintaining a positive mindest and learning to work well with others.
What are your impressions? Sherf's explanation about "consumer mindset" made me realise that I have to be really hungry and make the most out of it during this short time period as a customer/consumer. I have made a big investment in this and will only get a lot out of it if I put in the hardwork.
How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture? I love helping others when I am confident in what I know and what I am doing. I am also not afraid of asking questions to others if I am one or one. However, I wish to build my confidence in asking questions in a group through this EDA experience as I think asking questions is the fastest way to learn things. I will enjoy being engaged in this kind of welcoming and learning culture.
Have your expectations of EDA changed? If so, how? Yes. In addition to learn coding, I believe I will learn people skills by bulding relationships with others. I will also learn valuable skills to deal with stress and work related situation.
Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment? Does it make you nervous? This learning environment is completely new to me so I am excited but nervous at the same time. I look forward to learning a lot and increasing my confidence in many different aspects.
