What a responsive site is, and why responsiveness is important?

A responsive site is a website that fits the screen size of a user's device. Responsiveness is important as it allows all users to have a good viewing experience accross different types of devices they are using.

What mobile first design is, and why it's important?

Mobile first design is the concept of designing a website based on the size of mobile device screens and expanding to bigger screen devices. It is important due to the growth of mobile device users and it also allows designers to focus on the content of the website within limited spaces.

What frameworks are, and their pros and cons?

Frameworks are pre-designed templates of code (HTML, CSS, JS documents, etc.) which can be used to develop websites. This makes developers' life way easier as this saves them time as they don't have to build a website from scratch. However, this will not only restrict the flexibility of the website but also result in one website resembling another and also limit the developers learning.

What a wireframe is and why do we use it?

A wireframe is a basic visual structure of a website and provides the information hierarchy of the website and allows for brainstorming ideas amongst teams and clients.
