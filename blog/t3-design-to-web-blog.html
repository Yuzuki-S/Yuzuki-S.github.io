What a responsive site is, and why responsiveness is important?

A responsive site is a websitet that fits the screen size of a user's device. Responsiveness is important as it allows all users to have a good veiwing experience accross different types of devicesthey are using.

What mobile first design is, and why it's important?

Mobile first design is the concept of designing a website based on the size of mobile device screen and expanding to bigger screen devices. It is important due to the growth of mobile device users and it also allows designers to focus on the content of the website within limited spaces.

What frameworks are, and their pros and cons?

A good analogy for understanding HTML, CSS and the DOM is to think of the different components of being parts of a house. HTML is the structure of a house, describing the layout of the rooms. CSS is the style of the house, specifying the color of the walls, carpets and size and shape of the rooms. The DOM is like an architecture plan which shows each components' details. This will also allow to change the structure and the style of a house.
